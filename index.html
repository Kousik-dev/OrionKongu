About Event

ORION 2K23

ORION is the National level Technical Symposium, we made arrangements to participate the outside students of other institution through this symposium and to build a concrete bonding among them to share their technical and non technical exposure. The Prizes are awarded for the winners in each event.

This symposium started in 1998 as an amalgamation of many technical and entrepreneurship events held during the fest, including Paper Presentation, Debugging, Quiz, etc. The focus of ORION is to enhance the Technical and Managerial Skills of students to being a facilitator.

  • 10:00 AM - 12:30 AM Paper Presentation - Rules and regulation

    1.A team may contain maximum 1 to 3 members.

    2.Registration Fee Rs.200-for Each participant.

    3.Each presentation team will get 7 min for presentation + 03 min for Q & A.

    4.The presentation should be only in MS PowerPoint (ppt /pptx)

    5.Presentation time restrictions will be strictly followed.

    6.Select any topic relevant to recent trends and upcoming technologies.

    7.The deadline for presentation submission: 31.03.2023.

    8.Maximum number of 12 slides only allowed and must be in Times New Roman font completely.

    9.Select any topic relevant to recent trends and upcoming technologies.

    10.Must submit one hard copy of paper which contains Abstract, Introduction, Methodologies & Future works (if any), Conclusion. (Minimum two pages).

    11.File name: COLLEGE NAME_PARTICIPANT’S NAME_PRESENTATION (e.g., KEC_ARCHANA_PRESENTATION).

    The Presentation must be sent to: ramyad.21mca@kongu.edu
